About SWiTHER

For 12 weeks I spent over 500 hours engaged in learning something I knew completely nothing about. The app you're looking at is a culimnation of putting my life on hold to make a career change. It's also something I believe could really be something really cool

...around version 5 or 6, that is.

It's also the start of a really incredible passion that I feel I've developed. I love to program. Am I good at it? I don't know yet. But it's extremely fun. And frustrating. But mostly fun.

Oh yeah! That mugshot below is me. My name is Josh Stewart and I put together this app for you and your friends and family. Wanna have a fun way to visit new places? Wanna make it easier to pick where you want to go with your significant other tonight? Just open up the app and search a place or two, get some matches, and away you go!

This is just version one, and I had a little over nine days to work on it but I hope you enjoy it.

Future features I hope to include in newer versions:

  • Facebook/Google Login
  • Star Rating System for Likes
  • Calendar Integration for specific days
  • Notifications for matches
  • More functionality of your own data
  • Something else...
